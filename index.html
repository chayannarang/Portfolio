How I operate
1

Most product problems are sequencing problems.

The hardest decision is rarely what to build.

It's what to stop, what to defer and what to solve first.

2

Enterprise platforms scale on shared rules, not removed autonomy.

The objective isn't reducing autonomy.

It's reducing unnecessary complexity.

3

Every local optimization creates global complexity.

Good product judgement is deciding where standardization creates more value than flexibility.

4

Evidence beats opinion.

Customer feedback, behavioural analytics and production telemetry each tell part of the story.

Together they show you which problem is actually worth solving.
